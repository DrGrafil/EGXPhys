\[ r_{shift} = \sqrt{r_o^2+r_p^2 - 2 r_o r_p ( \cos(\phi_p) \cos(\phi_o) + \cos(\theta_p-\theta_o) * \sin(\phi_p) \sin(\phi_o))}\]
