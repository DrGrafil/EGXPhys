template<typename T >
T EGXMath::EllipseSemiMajorAxis ( const T  semiMinorAxisInm,
const T  eccentricity 
)

Finds the semi-major axis, \(a\), in meters of an ellipse with semi-minor axis \(b\), and eccentricity \(e\).

\[ a=\frac{b}{\sqrt{1-e^2}} \]

.

The semi-major axis of an ellipse is a line segment that starts at the center of the ellipse going the farthest point on the curve. It is half the length of the major axis and is at right angles with the semi-minor axis. It runs through one of the ellipses foci.

See https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Semi-major_and_semi-minor_axes , http://mathworld.wolfram.com/Ellipse.html
