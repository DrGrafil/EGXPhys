template<typename T >
T EGXPhys::ParallaxToAU ( const T  parallaxInArcseconds)

Calculates the distance, \(D\), of an object in astronomical units from its observed parallax, \(p\), in arcseconds.

\[ D=\dfrac{648000}{\pi}\dfrac{1}{p}\]

.

See http://astro.physics.uiowa.edu/ITU/glossary/stellar-parallax/ and https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stellar_parallax

Equation taken from http://astronomyonline.org/Science/Parallax.asp

Parameters
parallaxInArcseconds\( p\ (")\) Parallax of object in arcseconds. See http://coolcosmos.ipac.caltech.edu/cosmic_classroom/cosmic_reference/angular.html
Returns
\( D\ (au)\) Distance in astronomical units.
See also
ParallaxToParsec() for conversion into parsecs.
ParallaxToLightYear() for conversion into light years.
ParallaxToMeter() for conversion into meters.
