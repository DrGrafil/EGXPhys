Converts a 2D point represented in the Cartesian coordinate system \((x,y)\) to a representation in the Polar coordiante system \((r,\theta)\) .

\[ r = \sqrt{x^2+y^2} \]

\[ \theta = atan2(y,x) \]

.

See http://mathworld.wolfram.com/PolarCoordinates.html and https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Polar_coordinate_system

Parameters
x\( x\ (m)\) The \(x\) cartesian coordinate in meters.
y\( y\ (m)\) The \(y\) cartesian coordinate in meters.
polarR\( r\ (m)\) The \(r\) polar coordinate (polar radius) in meters.
polarAzimuthInRadians\( \theta\ (rad)\) The \(\theta\) polar coordinate (azimuth angle/polar angle) in radians.
