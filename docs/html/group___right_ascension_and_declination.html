Converts right ascensions \((\alpha)\) from hours \((RA_h)\), minutes \((RA_m)\) and seconds \((RA_s)\) to radians.

\[\alpha=\frac{pi}{180.0}(15 RA_h + \frac{RA_m}{4.0} + \frac{RA_s}{240.0})\]

See http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Right_ascension, http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sidereal_hour_angle.

Parameters
starMass\(m_{star}\ (kg)\) is the mass of the star.
referenceMass\(m_{ref}\ (kg)\) is the mass of the reference star.
Returns
\(M\)(dimensionless) Stellar mass. Mass ratio of a star compared to a reference star.
