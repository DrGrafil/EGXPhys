template<typename T >
T EGXMath::EllipseFocalLength ( const T  linearEccentricityInm)

Finds the focal length, \(f\), in meters of an ellipse with linear eccentricity, \(c\).

\[ f = 2c \]

.

The focal length is the distance betweem the two foci.
