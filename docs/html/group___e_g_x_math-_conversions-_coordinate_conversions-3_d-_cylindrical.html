Converts a 3D point represented in the Cylindrical coordiante system \((r_C,\theta_C,z_C)\) to a representation in the Cartesian coordinate system \((x,y,z)\).

\[ x = r_C\ cos(\theta_C) \]

\[ y = r_C\ sin(\theta_C) \]

\[ z = z_C \]

.

See http://mathworld.wolfram.com/CylindricalCoordinates.html and https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cylindrical_coordinate_system

Converts a 3D point represented in the Cartesian coordinate system \((x,y,z)\) to a representation in the Spherical coordiante system \((r,\theta,\phi)\). Note that \(\theta\) lays in the \(x\ y\) plane.

\[ r_S = \sqrt{x^2+y^2+z^2} \]

\[ \theta_S = arctan(\frac{y}{x}) \]

\[ \phi_S = arccos(\frac{z}{r}) \]

.

See http://mathworld.wolfram.com/SphericalCoordinates.html and https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spherical_coordinate_system

Parameters
cylindricalR\( r_C\ (m)\) The \(r_C\) cylindrical coordinate (cylindrical radius) in meters.
cylindricalAzimuthInRadians\( \theta_C\ (rad)\) The \(\theta_C\) cylindrical coordinate (azimuth angle) in radians.
cylindricalZ\( z_C\ (m)\) The \(z_C\) cylindrical coordinate in meters.
sphericalR\( r_S\ (m)\) The \(r_S\) spherical coordinate (spherical radius) in meters.
sphericalAzimuthInRadians\( \theta_S\ (rad)\) The \(\theta_S\) spherical coordinate (azimuth angle) in radians. Note that the azimuth angle is in the \(x\ y\) plane.
sphericalZenithInRadians\( \phi_S\ (rad)\) The \(\phi_S\) spherical coordinate (zenith angle) in radians. Note that the zenith angle is measured from the z-axis.
